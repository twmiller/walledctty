BBS Doors are external programs that the host BBS software can utilize to expand its features and offer additional diversions beyond the built-in message areas and file handling. Although various utility doors exist, including those with echo-message base handling (for systems without the feature built-in) and file utilities, the primary purpose of doors is to provide access to games! WalledCTTY currently offers a selection of games for its users to enjoy through BBS Doors. While almost all of the doors support ANSI/ASCII by default, some also provide the user with the option to use specific external clients that offer EGA or VGA graphics while playing the game.

Barren Realms Elite

Barren Realms Elite (BRE) is a popular BBS door game that was first released in 1994. BRE exists in a future world ravaged by nuclear fire, a desolate expanse stretching as far as the eye can see. Amidst this wasteland, the player assumes the mantle of a baron, locked in a fierce struggle with rival barons for supremacy over the blasted terrain. Only the strongest and most cunning will emerge victorious in this unforgiving landscape.

Land of Devastation

Land of Devastation is a text-based post-apocalyptic role-playing game from 1990. In the wake of a brutal nuclear apocalypse, a tenacious clan of human beings have managed to survive within the fortified confines of Sacre Base. These resourceful humans have devised a remarkable invention called the Puritron, a device capable of filtering out all radioactive contaminants from the atmosphere and restoring the toxic wasteland. Their hard-won achievement is snatched away by a band of mutants who steal the device. In the Land of Devestation (LOD), the player undertakes the daunting quest of tracking down the scattered remnants of the Puritron and returning them to Sacre Base, where the contraption can be restored.

Legend of the Red Dragon

Legend of the Red Dragon (LORD) is a text-based role-playing door game from 1989. You've found yourself in the middle of a town terrorized by a red dragon, and the only way out is to fight your way to the top. You'll be pitted against other players, each one hungry for the ultimate prize: the dragon's head on a platter. But to get there, you've gotta train hard and fight harder. Engage in brutal combat and earn the experience you need to level up. Challenge master Turgon himself, and prove your worth as a warrior. But be warned, with each level comes even tougher opponents and even more brutal masters. At level 12 the ultimate challenge awaits: the dragon will be waiting for you.

Legend of the Red Dragon 2

Legend of the Red Dragon 2 (LORD2) is a popular BBS door game from 1992 that builds upon the original "Legend of the Red Dragon" (LORD) game. Set in a medieval fantasy world, players create characters and engage in various activities such as fighting monsters, completing quests, and interacting with other players. With it's top-down roguelike view and multi-player support, LORD2 feels almost as much like a MUD as a sequel to LORD. LORD2 offers new features such as improved graphics, additional character classes, and a broader range of quests and challenges.

LORE

LORE is a fantasy role-playing game from 1996 with a roguelike-style interface, allowing players to navigate the world by moving around a graphical ANSI map. With over 50 cities and other establishments to explore, players can interact with various NPCs in each location. Lore also features combat, and the underlying character and combat routines were loosely based on the RoleMaster game system. Additionally, Lore is multi-node capable and supported real-time interaction between players.

Netrunner

Netrunner is a BBS door game that was released in 1992. The game is set in a cyberpunk-themed world, where players assume the role of a hacker attempting to infiltrate various computer systems. The game features turn-based gameplay, with players selecting actions such as hacking into databases, stealing files, and avoiding security measures. Netrunner gained a significant following due to its immersive gameplay and theme, and it is still played today on BBSs and emulated environments.

Operation Overkill II

Operation Overkill II is a BBS door game that was released in 1993. The game is a post-apocalyptic-themed, turn-based strategy game where players must navigate a harsh and unforgiving wasteland. The game features resource management, base building, and combat mechanics, as players attempt to gather resources, build up their base, and defend against enemy attacks. Operation Overkill II gained a significant following due to its immersive gameplay and unique theme, and it is still played today on BBSs and emulated environments.

The Pit

The Pit is a BBS door game from 1994 that offers players an arena-style fighting game. Players can create and train their own characters, and then compete in battles against AI controlled NPC and other players' characters. The game features turn-based combat mechanics and a variety of weapons and armor that can be acquired and upgraded throughout gameplay. Despite its age, "The Pit" is still enjoyed by players today on BBSs and emulated environments, including this one!.

Tradewars 2002

Tradewars 2002 is a classic BBS door game that was first released in the early 1990s. In the game, players assume the role of spaceship captains who must trade goods, mine asteroids, and battle enemy ships in order to earn money and advance their ranks. The game takes place in a persistent universe where multiple players can interact with each other, forming alliances or engaging in conflicts. The goal of the game is to become the richest and most powerful player in the universe. Tradewars 2002 is widely regarded as one of the most popular and influential BBS door games of all time, and it continues to have a dedicated fanbase to this day.
